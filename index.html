About

Gentle Monster

Founded in Seoul in 2011, Gentle Monster was created with a singular vision: to revolutionize eyewear through bold design and artistic innovation. Blending art, technology, and fashion, each piece is more than just eyewear — it’s a statement. From immersive store experiences to global collaborations with icons like Maison Margiela and Jennie of BLACKPINK, Gentle Monster continues to redefine what modern eyewear looks like. At Gentle Monster, we’re proud to share this vision — bringing you eyewear that challenges the norm and inspires a new way of seeing.
